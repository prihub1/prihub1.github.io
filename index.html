Priyal Mehta

UC Berkeley MIMS' 25'

As a journalism student, it is instilled in me that there is a voice unheard, a hero unsung. That there is a story beneath the noise that we call the news. ​ I believe in creating the most inclusive and accessible digital experiences we can. By taking each pixel seriously, we are designing for the unincluded, the marginalized, the disparaged. ​ An avid reader and and at heart, a storyteller. I loved listening to my mother's stories as I grew up and it is with that love and passion I try to craft consequential and worthwhile moments and adventures for the people around me. ​ I'll end with a quote - "Let's think the unthinkable, let's do the undoable. Let us prepare to grapple with the ineffable itself, and see if we may not eff it after all." - Douglas Adams
